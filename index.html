WHO AM I

Hi there, I'm Omirin Favour, a 15-year-old boy from the western part of Nigeria with dreams as vast as the African horizon. Let me take you on a journey through my world, where every day unfolds new possibilities and challenges.

Growing up, I have nurtured the interest of becoming a cybersecurity expert. As a result, I started my journey at a tender age of 15 to study Frontend engineering at AltSchool for me to understand the design and interaction of website. I grew up in a community where cybercrime prevails and youth are often unmasked as scammers. Hence, I chose to study at AltSchool that has a strong mission to teach youth the skills to make decent living. I intend using my skills as a Cybersecurity expert to fight cybercrime.

Apart from cybercrime in my society, I discovered that innocent people and companies have lost huge amount of money due to credit card and identity theft scams and Ever since I laid eyes on the vast realm of technology, I knew that I wanted to carve a path into the dynamic and ever-evolving field of cybersecurity. The idea of safeguarding digital spaces and combating cyber threats fascinated me, and I could not ignore the call to become a cybersecurity expert. This discovery rekindled my interest to become a cybersecurity expert. Hence, I learned Graphic Design, Animation and UI/UX Design from Coursera which I believe are necessary for my career.
